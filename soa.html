STUDENTS OUTCOMES ASSESSMENT FOR PAMANTASAN NG CABUYAO

This system is dedicated for Students of Pamantasan ng Cabuyao. The primary function of this system are student assessment, creating of exams, curriculum management, curriculum mapping. The goal of this system is to continuously improve the academic curriculums of Pamantasan ng Cabuyao.

Features

  • Assessments of Student Outcomes
  • Test Bank Management
  • Programs Management
  • Courses Management
  • Manage Academic Curriculum
  • Manage Student Grades
  • Curriculum Mapping to Student Outcomes
